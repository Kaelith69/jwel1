Overview

At VastraVeda Abharana, every jewellery piece is packed with utmost care and goes through multiple quality checks before dispatch. Hence, we do not offer returns or refunds. We provide an exchange option only for items that are damaged or defective on arrival.

Proof Required

To be eligible for an exchange due to damage or defect, customers must share an unboxing video (showing the parcel being opened from the moment of opening) as proof within 48 hours of delivery. The unboxing video helps us verify the condition of the item at delivery and process the exchange quickly.

Eligibility for Exchange

  • Exchanges are available only for damaged or defective items received at delivery.
  • Provide the unboxing video (as described above) within 48 hours of delivery to start the exchange process.
  • Custom, engraved, or bespoke items are non-exchangeable unless faulty on arrival.

How to Request an Exchange

  1. Contact us as soon as possible via the Contact page and include your order number, a short description of the issue, and a link to or attachment of the unboxing video (required within 48 hours of delivery).
  2. Our team will review the video and details, and if approved, we'll provide return/exchange instructions and an authorization number.
  3. Please retain the original packaging and use a tracked shipping service for any return shipments; keep your tracking number until the exchange is completed.

Refunds

We do not offer refunds as a general policy. Refunds will only be considered in exceptional cases at our discretion (for example, when an exchange is not possible). If a refund is approved, it will be processed to the original payment method after inspection.

Questions?

Contact our support team at the Contact page and include your order number so we can assist quickly.
