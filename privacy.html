Privacy Policy

Effective date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of October 30, 2025.

Introduction

VastraVeda Jewelleries ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors and customers. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Contact and account information (name, email, phone, shipping and billing addresses) you provide when placing an order or creating an account.
  • Payment information provided to our payment processor (we do not store full card details on our servers).
  • Order and transaction details (products purchased, order history).
  • Technical information collected automatically (IP address, device information, browser and usage data) via cookies and similar technologies.
  • User-generated content (reviews, messages to customer support), if you provide it.

How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide and fulfil your orders, process payments, and manage shipping.
  • Communicate with you about orders, returns/exchanges, and customer support requests.
  • Improve our website, personalize content, and prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Send marketing communications when you have opted in; you may unsubscribe at any time.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable core site functionality, analyze usage, and support advertising. You can control cookie settings in your browser, though disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Third-party services

We may share data with service providers who perform services on our behalf (payment processors, shipping partners, analytics providers). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and only use it for the services they provide to us.

Data retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to restrict or object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us via the Contact page.

Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect personal information, but no online transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have such data, contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top indicates when the policy was last updated. We will notify customers of material changes where required.

Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
